Design of scour protection for the bridge piers Mascara, Algeria

CPS was retained to prepare an analysis of piers scour of a new viaduct in Mascara, Algeria.

My role:

A HEC-RAS model was prepared for the 250-, and 500-year events and used to assess piers of viaduct scour and erosion risk.

A 3D profile plot of the computed water surface elevation

The scour depth was predicted in cohesive and cohesionless soil, depending on a pier location. Two methodology of pier scour was compared:

 According to SETRA (French code)

 According to FWHA HEC-18 formula (U.S. Department of Transportation Federal Highway Administration)

Further, riprap protection was designed.

Example of a cross section with a bridge of a hydraulic model (HEC-RAS)

Outcome:

The HEC-RAS model was used to demonstrate that the piers of the viaduct were exposed to the processes of water erosion. Then the riprap design protection was provided.
